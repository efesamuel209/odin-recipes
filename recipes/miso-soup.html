Miso Soup

Description

Miso Soup is is a traditional Japanese soup consisting of miso paste mixed into dashi stock

With various delicious ingredients, it warms both your body and your heart

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste.
  4. Stir in tofu.
  5. Separate the layers of green onions, and add them to the soup.
  6. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
